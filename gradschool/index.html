Projects

Factorie

FACTORIE is a probabilistic programming framework implemented as a software library in Scala. Providing users with a succinct framework for creating abritrary factor graphs with tied parameters, estimating parameters, and performing inference.

Rexa

Rexa is a digital library and search engine covering the computer science research literature and the people who create it. Rexa aims to facilitate research progress and collaboration by providing efficient browsing, search, associations and analysis among papers, people, organizations, venues and research communities.

Textual Entailment

At UIUC, I worked with Dan Roth developing a system for textual entailment---i.e., determining whether the meaning of a given text passage, entails that of another. We propose an architecture in Sammons et al. (2009) for accomplishing this task.

In addition to my contributions to the system, I also created a visualization to enable us to quickly see what each of our various components are outputting. (note: try selecting only the Quantities and LCS (Identity) as many components may be unavailable).
